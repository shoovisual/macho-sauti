Measuring, evaluating and learning from what we do

A comprehensive monitoring and evaluation plan is being developed for the Macho Sauti programme, which will monitor progress and measure results against indicators and targets. A dedicated team of Monitoring, Evaluation and Learning (MEL) specialists will work closely with biological and social scientists within CABI, partners and country implementation teams to test assumptions and evaluate outcomes and impacts

CABI and partners have learnt from previous programmes the value of using both quantitative and qualitative methods to ensure that a full range of evidence is captured, as well as a mix of internal and third-party evaluations to ensure impartiality.
